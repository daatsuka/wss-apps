Privacy Policy

WSS Apps — Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WSS Apps ("we", "us") handles data in connection with our apps distributed on the Atlassian Marketplace, including AI Test Coverage for Jira and MCP Gateway — AI Agent Audit for Jira (the "Apps").

1. Where your data lives

The Apps are built on the Atlassian Forge platform and run entirely inside Atlassian's cloud infrastructure. We do not operate any external servers, and no data ever leaves the Atlassian platform. Our Apps qualify for Atlassian's "Runs on Atlassian" program.

2. Data we process and store

AI Test Coverage

  • Reads the summary and description of Jira issues you choose to generate test cases for.
  • Stores generated test cases and their execution status in Forge app storage, scoped to your Atlassian site.
  • Issue text is processed by Atlassian's Forge LLM service to generate test cases. It is not used to train models and is not sent to any third party outside the Atlassian platform.

MCP Gateway — AI Agent Audit

  • Records an audit trail of issue activity (create, update, delete, comment) in your Jira site, including the Atlassian account ID of the actor, timestamps, issue keys, and a short description of the change.
  • Stores a list of Atlassian account IDs that a site administrator explicitly registers as AI agent accounts.
  • All audit data is stored in Forge app storage, scoped to your Atlassian site, and is only accessible to your site's administrators.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any customer data on systems outside of Atlassian.
  • We do not use analytics trackers, advertising identifiers, or cookies.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.

4. Data retention and deletion

App data is retained in Forge storage for as long as the App is installed. Uninstalling an App from your site permanently removes its stored data in accordance with Atlassian's Forge data lifecycle policies. You may also request deletion of specific data by contacting us.

5. Security

Data at rest and in transit is protected by Atlassian's platform-level security controls. See Atlassian Trust for details.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because all data resides within your own Atlassian site, your site administrator can fulfil most requests directly. For anything else, contact us.
